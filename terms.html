VideoGen is an operator-controlled workflow for generating, assembling, and publishing AI-assisted videos.

The service is intended for authorized channel operators. Users are responsible for ensuring that uploaded content complies with the rules of each publishing platform.

When a platform account is connected, VideoGen uses the granted permissions only to authenticate the account, prepare uploads, create drafts or posts, and check publication status.

Content responsibility

The operator is responsible for reviewing generated video, captions, descriptions, tags, thumbnails, and disclosures before public publication.
