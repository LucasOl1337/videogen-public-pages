VideoGen Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

VideoGen is a local workflow used to prepare and publish AI-assisted videos to channels selected by the operator.

Information processed

When a user authorizes a connected platform such as TikTok or YouTube, VideoGen may process platform account identifiers, profile information allowed by the user, upload permissions, video metadata, and publication status returned by the platform API.

Use of information

Information is used only to authenticate the selected channel, prepare uploads, submit video drafts or posts, and track publication status. VideoGen does not sell personal information.

Storage

Operational data is stored locally by the operator. Access tokens and refresh tokens are treated as credentials and should not be published or shared.

Data deletion

Users can revoke access from the connected platform account settings. The operator can delete local VideoGen tokens, manifests, and channel records on request.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the operator through the GitHub account LucasOl1337.
